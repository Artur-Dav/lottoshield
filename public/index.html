Complete lottery security

Theft of scratcher tickets can result in tens of thousands of dollars in losses before the store owner is aware of the problem; but using handwritten logs and back-office software can be time-consuming and often inaccurate.

LottoShield is a patent-pending technology that eliminates the need for handwritten logs. It automatically tracks changes in scratcher inventory and reconciles with sales, alerting you at the first sign of a problem so you can take steps to prevent further loss.

How it works

Install

Connect the LottoShield device to your existing scanner and POS.

You'll also need to install the LottoShield Sales Tracker on your back-office computer — a light-weight application that will track lottery sales.

Scan

Your employees take inventory by scanning your scratchers at the end of their shift using the same scanner they use for sales.

LottoShield automatically calculates your remaining inventory and reconciles with sales to detect missing tickets.

Reconcile

LottoShield receives daily reports from your state lottery and checks to make sure your draw sales and lottery payouts match official records.

LottoShield also uses these reports to monitor the scratcher packs in your stockroom and to detect any scratcher packs that have been activated but are not being sold.

Investigate

Daily emails will show you the overages or shortages on scratchers, draw tickets, and payouts from the previous day.

The dashboard makes it easy to investigate issues. Detailed reports and timelines help you pinpoint root causes quickly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does LottoShield know when my packs finish?
Yes. LottoShield will automatically detect when your scratcher packs finish and when new packs start selling.
Can I sell multiple packs of the same game at the same time?
Yes. If you are selling multiple facings of the same game at a particular time, LottoShield will automatically detect this and monitor both packs. You don't need to do anything else.
Can I track packs that are in the stock room?
Yes. LottoShield automatically checks your state lottery report every day. If there are activated packs that are not on the sales floor (i.e. missing from the stock room) you will be notified.
How will I get notified if there's an issue?
Every morning you'll receive an email summary of the previous day highlighting any issues. You can also customize to be notified by SMS or email right away if we detect an issue.
How can I investigate an issue?
LottoShield provides a time frame during which the loss happened. You can check your security footage during this period to find the cause of the discrepancy and take action to prevent further loss.
What if an issue gets resolved?
You can easily click 'Resolve' next to any item on a report and the amount will be removed from your monthly totals.
